Math Game

Controls

Use Arrow Keys: Up & Down to move through the lanes.

Enter numbers using the number keys.

Use Enter or Spacebar to confirm entered numbers.

Backspace to delete incorrect number input.

Game Rules

Enemies will appear in 5 different lanes from the left charging towards the player to the right.

Player must be in the same lane the enemy to defeat it.

Once in the same lane, player must input correct answer of the corresponding enemy's lane.

When player is sure they have the correct answer they can confirm input.

If answer in correct, the enemy will be defeated and will disappear. If not, the corresponding enemy's equation will change.

If an enemy gets past the player, the game will be over.

If the player can manage 5 levels of enemies, they will win the game.

Good Luck and Have Fun!
